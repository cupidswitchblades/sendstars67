How to purchase a gift?
You can purchase any collectible gift using Telegram Stars or TON. Once purchased, the NFT is instantly linked to your account and appears in your Telegram profile under the "Gifts" tab. Confirmation usually takes 30-60 seconds.
Transaction safety & Security
All transactions are processed through secure smart contracts on the TON blockchain. This ensures that you either receive your digital asset or your funds remain in your wallet. Portals never has access to your private keys.
Where can I find my NFT?
Once the transaction is complete, open your Telegram Profile -> click the 'Gifts' button. If you don't see it immediately, try pulling down to refresh the interface or check the 'Hidden' tab in your settings.
